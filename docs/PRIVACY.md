# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled by the Kawerify Tech Passwords utility. Because we prioritize user security above all, our architecture is built around absolute data isolation.

---

## 1. Zero Server-Side Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your passwords, credentials, or personal information.**
- This application runs entirely client-side in your web browser.
- There are no background server connections, databases, or API tracking calls that capture the passwords you generate, type, or save.
- No analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics or other advertising trackers) are embedded in this application, ensuring zero network monitoring of user activities.

## 2. Browser LocalStorage Usage

- When you use the "Save Password" function, the selected password, label, and configuration parameters are stored locally inside your browser's `LocalStorage` cache.
- `LocalStorage` is sandbox-isolated by the browser under the same-origin policy, meaning other websites cannot read or access this data.
- This data remains on your physical hardware device at all times. It is never uploaded to Vercel, Kawerify Tech, or any third-party storage.

## 3. Data Deletion and Clear-out

- You can delete individual items or clear your entire saved history at any time using the "Clear All" button in the history drawer interface.
- Clearing your browser's site data, cookies, or local cache for this domain will permanently delete all saved passwords. We recommend keeping backups of crucial credentials.

## 4. External Links

Our application links to external resources, specifically `kawerifytech.com`. If you click these links, you will navigate to external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those domains when visiting.
